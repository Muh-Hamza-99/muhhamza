After Summer 2024, I was going to be looking for co-ops/internships in the Winter and Fall of 2025, based on my schedule.

When looking for a co-op for the Winter term, I was hit with my first big interview fail. After completing the online assessment for Super.com, I received an interview invite. I think just the sheer dopamine hit when I saw that email was the end of me.

Although I had heard their interviews were focused around creating and modifying API routes (which I was pretty good at), the thought of not getting the offer made me brain fart in the interview. Despite keeping hopes high, I didn't hear back.

Shortly after though, I received an interview from Nokia. I went into the interview not knowing what to expect but with the mindset of simply doing the interview and not thinking about the consequences.

It ended up being a literal resume 'grill', as I started sweating during the interview after talking so much. The interviewer asked questions about the interesting parts of my resume, asking me about more details, what I knew, etc.

When we wrapped up the interview, I had the same feeling as Super.com. However, to my surprise, I received an email roughly 2-3 hours later saying I got the offer. I was ecstatic because, one, I had finally got a software internship and, two, I was going to Ottawa (I know, but it was still a new city).

While at Nokia, I started Leetcode. I watched Neetcode, learnt the basic techniques, and by the end of the co-op term, I had done around 100 problems, over 50 of which were medium/hard.

I received a return offer to Nokia as a DevOps Engineer, which I declined in hopes of __something better__. I also reached the final round for Shopify, which really bummed me.
