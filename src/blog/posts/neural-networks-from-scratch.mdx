# Neural Networks from Scratch in C++

Building a tiny network by hand is one of the fastest ways to demystify backpropagation. You own every allocation, every loop, and every gradient.

## Why C++

You get explicit control over memory layout and numerical hot paths. That matters when you are experimenting with SIMD, custom activations, or odd batch sizes.

## Outline

1. Represent tensors as flat arrays plus shape metadata.
2. Implement forward ops: matmul, bias add, ReLU, softmax.
3. Derive backward passes or use finite differences for sanity checks.
4. Wrap a minimal SGD step and log loss over epochs.
